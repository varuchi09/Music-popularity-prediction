About Us

PricePredict is built to simplify real estate decisions. Whether you're a buyer, builder or developer, our tool helps you estimate house prices accurately and interactively.

Developed by Mayank Garg and Varuchi Maurya, undergraduates from ADGIPS, New Delhi, with a shared passion for ML, design and practical AI tools.

  • 🤝 Get AI-backed negotiation suggestions
